About this item

Green Moong Dal Split is a good source of protein, especially for vegetarians and vegans. When eaten along with rice or roti (cereal) it provides complete protein like milk or non-vegetarian foods. It is a good source of certain micronutrients and also a great source of dietary fibre. Our 24 Mantra Organic Green Moong Dal Split is unadulterated and unpolished. It is 100 % Organic, grown without synthetic pesticides and grown without synthetic GMOs.
